A timeless Modern Tudor home with refined chateau influences, meticulously crafted for elevated everyday living. Thoughtful proportions, steep rooflines, and architectural detailing create a striking first impression, while the interior is designed to feel calm, bright, and effortlessly livable. A dedicated pantry, functional mudroom, and a well planned laundry area add everyday convenience without compromising design.

The private primary suite offers a quiet retreat, complemented by a spa inspired bathroom experience and generous storage. Secondary spaces are positioned for flexibility, whether for family, guests, or a quiet office setting. The home is positioned to create a quiet sense of arrival, with natural separation from the street and room to enjoy outdoor living without feeling exposed. This setting provides the perfect canvas for a timeless estate lifestyle, with the freedom to create future outdoor enhancements while preserving the peaceful, private atmosphere that defines the experience.
